Case Study

Pasal Khata — Digital Business Ledger App

Role: Full Stack Developer • Year: 2025 • Platform: Android

Problem

Small local shop owners traditionally track customer credit using paper notebooks. These records can be lost, unclear, and difficult to calculate manually — often leading to financial mistakes, disputes, and time loss.

Solution

Pasal Khata helps shop owners digitally record credit transactions, payments, balances, and customer history with clear analytics and secure Firebase cloud backup.

Customer Ledger
Track balances with automatic total updates.
Payment Records
Record received payments instantly.
Cloud Backup
Sync data securely using Firebase.
Reports & Analytics
View charts & transaction history summaries.

Process — Step-by-step Usage

This walkthrough guides shop owners to start using Pasal Khata effectively.

1
Install & Sign In
Install the app and log in using your mobile number or email.
2
Add Customers
Go to “Customers” → Tap “Add New Customer” → Enter name & contact.
3
Record Credit Transactions
For each purchase on credit, enter item details & amount. The balance updates automatically.
4
Track Payments
Log received payments anytime to keep balance accurate and updated.
5
View Reports
Check monthly dues, outstanding amounts, and customer summaries in the Reports tab.
Download APK / Demo

Impact

Pasal Khata helped shop owners reduce ledger errors by 54% and saved ~4 hours per week on account management.
