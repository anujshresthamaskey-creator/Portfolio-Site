Case Study

MealMate — Meal Planning & Recipe App

Role: Product Designer & Developer • Year: 2024 • Platform: Android

Problem

Many users struggle to plan meals, manage grocery lists, and track nutrition in one place. Small shop owners and users alike often need a simple, offline-capable solution with sync.

Solution

MealMate is a lightweight Android app built with Java and Firebase. It offers meal plans, recipes, grocery lists, food logging, and geolocation to discover nearby stores.

Meals & Plans
Create daily/weekly meal plans and reusable recipes.
Grocery Lists
Auto-generate shopping lists from recipes.
Authentication
Secure signup & login with Firebase Auth.
Real-time Sync
Cloud sync with offline-first design.

Process — Step by step guide

Below is a guided walkthrough a user will follow to get started with MealMate. You can use the sidebar to jump between steps.

1
Install & Sign up
Download the app from Play Store or install APK. Create an account using email or sign in with Google. Accept permissions for location if you want store discovery.
2
Create Your First Meal Plan
Tap "New Plan" → choose days → add recipes from the library or create a custom recipe. Save and review weekly overview.
3
Generate Grocery List
From a meal plan, tap "Generate List" to convert ingredients into a consolidated shopping list. You can check-off items during shopping and sync across devices.
4
Track Intake & Discover Stores
Log meals to track nutrition. Use the "Nearby" tab to find stores; the app uses geolocation and shows directions.
5
Settings & Backup
Enable automatic backup to Firebase, manage account settings, and export lists as CSV if needed.

Impact & Metrics

After launch, MealMate reached 5,000 installs, 72% weekly retention, and reduced average grocery time by 18% in a small study.
